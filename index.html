    Campsite Cleanup

    Our initial effort to affect change in the Portland Homeless community has been a resounding success. It has thus far been limited in scope due to funds and volunteers, but what we've been able to do has been met with great enthusiasm. The premise is this: we distribute garbage bags to some of the camps in the area and let them know when we'll be back to pick them up. The campers take the initiative to clean up their space, we merely provide a pickup service!

    Would you like to know more?

    We have partnered with several local organizations in this effort, including XXX, xxx, XXX, and the Union Gospel Mission. Union Gospel Mission already is well known in the homeless community, so we began by accompanying their "Rescue" missions to build some credibility and interest. XXX garbage company was approached and very graciously has allowed us to drop off bagged garbage at no cost. This is an operation that can easily be scaled up as resources (funds/volunteers) become available! One such expansion plan is to provide interested campers with bicycle mounted trailers to tow garbage cans around to cleanup other sites.

    Thermal Blankets

    The first in a planned series of products we intend to create and distribute to members of the homeless community. With the cold/rainy season fast approaching, the need for blankets will increase dramatically. Our development team has designed a blanket that is an excellent insulator, waterproof, and something we can produce inexpensively. The idea is to provide interested locals with short term opportunities for employment in the production of the blankets - maybe longer term in the future! Union Gospel Mission has agreed to help with the distribution of blankets to needy campers.

Our Mission

Fruitful Spirit is a non-profit 501(c)3 organization ministering to the homeless and hurting in our community. Our mission is to create viable long term home and work alternatives for people who are not permitted, willing or able to utilize the options existing in the community today.
